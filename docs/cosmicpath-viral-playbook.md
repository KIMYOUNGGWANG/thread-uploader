# CosmicPath Viral Sprint Playbook

## Purpose

CosmicPath 바이럴은 댓글 상담이 아니라 질문을 작게 정리하게 만드는 참여 루프다.

Primary mechanics:
- `self-classification`: A/B/C, 연락/대기/축소/보류, 버팀형/이동형/준비형 중 하나를 고르게 한다.
- `saveable`: 저장 가능한 checklist, decision tool, 판정표, 순서표를 만든다.
- `quiet contrarian`: 예언형 운세 대신 행동선 정리라는 관점을 차분하게 뒤집는다.
- `friend share`: 같은 고민을 하는 친구에게 보내고 싶은 상황 정리로 만든다.

## Manual swipe-file seed workflow

수동 레퍼런스는 30-50개를 모아 시작한다.

Collect:
- relationship timing posts that make readers choose a side
- career anxiety posts with A/B/C or checklist structures
- saveable decision tool posts
- quiet contrarian posts about overthinking, timing, and choice
- friend-share posts that do not require the creator to answer replies

Reject:
- "comment and I will answer" posts
- partner-mind guarantee posts
- future certainty or 100% prediction posts
- generic motivation posts with no next action
- posts that only work if the operator replies manually

## 7-day sprint matrix

Generate 28 posts:
- 7 `self-classification` posts
- 7 `saveable` posts
- 7 `quiet contrarian` posts
- 7 `friend share` posts

Each post gets one primary target:
- replies for self-classification
- saves for saveable tools
- profile visits or report starts for quiet contrarian
- shares/reposts for friend-share

## Decision rules

Double down when:
- reply rate is meaningfully higher than baseline and comments are mostly short choices, not support requests
- click rate or report start rate improves on linked posts
- paid conversion or strong waitlist signal appears from one mode

Revise format when:
- views are fine but click rate and report starts stay flat
- comments become long support requests
- quality fail rate exceeds 20%
- posts feel repetitive across first lines or structures

Revise offer when:
- posts get saves/replies but no report starts or paid signal
- users engage with the problem but ignore the CTA

Pause when:
- no mode creates replies, saves, shares, profile visits, or report starts after 28 posts
- the only working angle requires prediction, partner-mind claims, or reply-heavy labor

## Operator guardrails

- Do not require answering every comment.
- Do not promise personal diagnosis in comments.
- Do not use auto-reply, auto-DM, auto-follow, or artificial engagement.
- Treat viral attention and report conversion as separate metrics.

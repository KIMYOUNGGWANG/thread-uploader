# Final Scope Fidelity

## User Intent

The product is an internal marketing workspace for promoting the user's own products. It should not present itself as a public SaaS or as CosmicPath-only.

## Scope Match

- CosmicPath remains supported as one product profile.
- Generic products get neutral product growth defaults.
- The UI now uses Portfolio Growth OS and product language on login, product list, settings, and dashboard surfaces.
- The create modal and generic settings placeholders use neutral product examples, so CosmicPath stays a product fixture rather than the default surface identity.
- Product Profile and Active Experiment are first-class config fields.
- Product Growth quality checks and prompt guidance support non-CosmicPath products.
- Related-account discovery supports non-CosmicPath product growth without hardcoded career/saju seed or scoring defaults.
- Docs describe internal owned products and 7-day evidence sprint operation.

## Out Of Scope Not Added

- No public onboarding surface.
- No payment, plan, seat, or tenant management surface.
- No automatic TikTok upload.
- No scraping or auto engagement behavior.
- No destructive migration or production data overwrite.
